How I approach marketing operations

Strong marketing operations is not just about launching campaigns faster. It is about building a system where audience logic, data flow, lifecycle transitions, CRM alignment, reporting fields, and QA checks are clear enough to support scale. For B2B teams using Marketo, Salesforce, 6sense, and Salesloft, the strongest results usually come from a simple operating principle: build the right foundation, document the logic, test the flow, and optimize based on real performance data.
