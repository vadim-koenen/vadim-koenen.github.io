Positioning

Not AI content volume. Context-to-asset execution.

This system is built for targeted GTM work where the hard part is not producing more words. The hard part is translating a company, role, operating pain, and proof record into a page that feels specific, credible, and source-faithful.

AI accelerates the research synthesis, mapping, and first-pass drafting. The operating discipline is human: claims are checked, experience is mapped conservatively, private data stays out, and live prospect links stay private unless approved.

Workflow

From target context to usable GTM assets.

Target input

Start with a target account, role, job description, or buying committee context.

Context analysis

Extract company priorities, role pain points, GTM motion, likely stakeholders, and operating constraints.

Proof mapping

Map the request to source-backed experience, metrics, platform fluency, and limits on what should not be claimed.

Page generation

Generate a focused landing page, messaging map, CTA structure, and optional supporting resume variant.

QA and deployment

Check links, responsive layout, claim accuracy, metadata, privacy guardrails, and deployment package readiness.

Follow-up content

Turn the same source map into email follow-up, LinkedIn-safe summaries, interview prep, or stakeholder notes.

Ethics and guardrails

Personalized does not mean invasive.

The engine is designed for legitimate professional positioning. It uses public context, user-provided role information, and source-backed professional proof. It does not manufacture relationships or automate private-channel behavior.

  • No fake personalization.
  • No fake engagement.
  • No scraped private data.
  • No credential, cookie, or browser-session use.
  • Approval before exposing live prospect links.
  • Source-faithful positioning only.
